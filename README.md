## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze growth of Electric Vehicles over time
* Identify top EV manufacturers and market leaders
* Compare BEV vs PHEV adoption
* Understand pricing vs electric range relationship
* Explore regional EV distribution

---

## 📁 Dataset Information

* **Source:** Electric Vehicle Population Data
* **Key Features:**

  * Make & Model
  * Model Year
  * Electric Range ⚡
  * Base MSRP 💰
  * County 🌍
  * EV Type (BEV / PHEV)

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### 🚗 Market Leaders

* Tesla dominates EV market share significantly
* Nissan and Chevrolet follow with a large gap

### 📈 EV Growth Trend

* Rapid adoption after 2015
* Strong dominance of Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs)

### ⚡ EV Type Distribution

* BEVs account for ~75%+ of total EVs
* PHEVs form a smaller segment

### 🌍 Regional Insights

* King County leads in EV registrations
* Urban areas show higher EV adoption

### 💰 Price vs Range Analysis

* Higher range → higher price (positive correlation)
* Some models offer good range at moderate cost
